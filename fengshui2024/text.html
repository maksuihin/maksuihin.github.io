''Those Who Play with Fire Will Perish by It'' Sui-hin Mak (set: $chi to 50)(set: $note to 0)(set: $visited_s_look to false)(set: $read_s to false)(set: $sw_deciphered to false) [[Start Game>>|Southern Palace]] Feng shui numerology considers fate in 60-year eras, divided into 3 scores. It was traditionally described that the Year of Dragon 2024 marks the beginning of the Score of Fire (2024-2043), which may be evidenced by the increased level of conflicts, wars, social upheavals, violence, pandemics, polarisation, destruction and chaos in recent years. In order to pray for good luck in the star-crossed Score of Fire, feng shui installations associated with the five elements are constructed and placed in the corresponding directions to harmonise or counteract malignant celestial effects. Remember that chi is the vital force, and you should find ways to conserve, augment or replenish your own chi. In this game, you will navigate and explore this field of installations. Notebook pages are scattered in the field and you may read them to understand more about my art practice. Like life, the objective of the game would not be expressly announced when you were born. I imagine that you would eventually discover the meaning of the game. I chose to submit the dissertation in the form of an interactive game because I believe that artists should not dictate the meanings of exhibited artworks, rather, art in contemporary society is a social product, where both the artists and the audience interact to (de-/re-)construct and (mis-/re-)interpret each other’s ideas. I believe that art has to be experienced. The playful presentation is to contrast with the seriousness and heaviness of the themes that run through my art practice, employed as a result of societal taboo, self censorship or political correct language to avoid offending people devoted to different cultural beliefs, consequently as a form of absurd humour.
