MAK, Sui-hin | 麥 瑞顯

愛玩視覺、文字和音符遊戲。港大園境建築畢業，香港主權移交前最後一個寒冬出生。 詩文散見於《明報》、《字花》、《香港作家》、《聲韻詩刊》、《大頭菜文藝》等。 實驗電影〈tofu〉曾於大英博物館放映。

Loves playing games on visuals, poetry, words and musical notes.
HKU landscape architecture graduate. Born last winter before Hong Kong handover. Sometimes contributes to local literary zines. Directed experimental film “tofu”, screened British Museum.
