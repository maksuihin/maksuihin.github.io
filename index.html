MAK, Sui-hin 麥瑞顯

BA(Landscape Studies) HKU; MFA Oxford expected (2024)

Sui-hin is a Hong Kong artist. His artworks address identity, science and technology, trauma, language, folk beliefs and modern lifestyles. His works encompass installation, painting, poetry, printmaking, sound art, experimental ﬁlm, photography etc. Some of his art prints are in private collections in Hong Kong, Australia, Taiwan and the US. In 2019, his experimental film ‘tofu’ was screened at the British Museum.

香港藝術家。創作探討身份、科技、創傷、語言、民間信仰、摩登新生活等主題。創作媒介涵蓋裝置藝術、繪畫、新詩、版畫、聲音藝術、實驗電影及攝影等。版畫作品於本港、澳洲、台灣、美國獲私人收藏。實驗短片〈tofu〉在2019年於大英博物館放映。
