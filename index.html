MAK, Sui-hin | 麥 瑞顯

麥瑞顯畢業於港大園境建築，愛玩視覺、文字和音符遊戲。
近年的創作交織影像和聲音藝術，邀請你張開耳目感受身邊日常， 反思現代社會和大地和自己的關係。詩文散見於本地報刊， 實驗電影〈tofu〉在2019年於大英博物館放映。

Mak Sui Hin graduated from HKU landscape architecture. He loves playing with visuals, poetry and musical notes.
His creative outputs in recent years weave images and sound art, inviting you to open your eyes and ears to feel everyday surroundings. He wants to reflect with you on the relationship between the contemporary society, the land, and themselves. Sometimes he also contributes to local literary zines. In 2019, his experimental film “tofu” was screened at the British Museum.
