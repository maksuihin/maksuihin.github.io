Sui-hin MAK 麥瑞顯

BA(Landscape Studies) HKU; MFA Oxford expected (2024)

Sui-hin is a Hong Kong artist and poet. By cross-pollinating with sono­dynamic experi­ments and print­making, he prays that the world will get well soon. He imag­ines art as a medium of memory, resonance, recovery and peace.

香港藝術家及詩人。以聲動力實驗和版畫交叉授粉，祈願世界早日康復。將藝術想像為記憶、共鳴、療癒及和平的媒介。
