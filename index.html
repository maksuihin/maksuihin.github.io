MAK, Sui-hin 麥瑞顯

麥瑞顯（阿顯），香港藝術家，愛玩視覺、文字和聲音遊戲。
畢業於港大園境建築。牛津大學藝術碩士學生。創作探討身份、科技、末世景象、創傷、語言、忌諱、民間信仰、摩登新生活等主題。創作媒介涵蓋裝置藝術、繪畫、新詩、版畫、聲音藝術、實驗電影及攝影等。版畫作品於本港、澳洲、台灣、美國獲私人收藏。實驗短片〈tofu〉在2019年於大英博物館放映。

Mak Sui Hin, a Hong Kong artist playing with visuals, words and musical notes.
He studied landscape architecture at HKU. Master of Fine Art student at the University of Oxford. His artworks address identity, science and technology, doomsday scenarios, trauma, language, taboos, folk beliefs and modern lifestyles. His works encompass installation, painting, poetry, printmaking, sound art, experimental ﬁlm, photography etc. Some of his art prints are in private collections in Hong Kong, Australia, Taiwan and the US. In 2019, his experimental film ‘tofu’ was screened at the British Museum.
