MAK, Sui-hin | 麥 瑞顯

麥瑞顯（阿顯），香港藝術創作者，愛玩視覺、文字和聲音遊戲。
近年以創作邀請你反思現代社會和大地和自己的關係。 畢業於港大園境建築，有時寫新詩。二零二三年將於圓圈交叉繼續攻讀藝術，待公佈。 創作探討身份、科技、末世景象、創傷、語言、忌諱、民間信仰、摩登新生活等主題。 版畫作品於本港、澳洲、台灣、美國獲私人收藏。 實驗短片〈tofu〉在2019年於大英博物館放映。

Mak Sui Hin, a Hong Kong artist playing with visuals, words and musical notes.
His recent creative outputs aim at reflecting with you on the relationship between the contemporary society, the land, and yourself. He graduated from HKU landscape architecture and sometimes writes poems. His artworks address themes such as identity, science and technology, doomsday scenarios, trauma, language, taboos, folk beliefs and modern lifestyles. Some of his art prints are in private collections in Hong Kong, Australia, Taiwan and the US. In 2019, his experimental film ‘tofu’ was screened at the British Museum.
