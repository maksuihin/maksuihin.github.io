Sui-hin MAK 麥瑞顯

Sui-hin Mak (b. 1996, Hong Kong) is an inter­dis­ci­plinary artist-poet working with moving-image instal­lations, print­­making, and per­for­mance.

► portfolio 2022 作品集

Mak has ex­hib­it­ed, per­formed and pub­lished in Hong Kong (2016-) and Sydney (2017), and grad­u­ated from the Uni­versity of Hong Kong in 2018 with a bach­e­lor's degree in land­scape archi­tecture. Mak’s short ﬁlm ‘tofu’ was screened at the British Mu­se­um in 2019. Mak is set to graduate from the Oxford MFA pro­gramme in June 2024.

Mak’s sono­dynamic experi­ments explore a float­ing sense of lim­i­nal be­long­ing, cross-pol­li­nat­ing folk­lore, com­e­dy, trau­ma, and tech­nology.  Mak prays that the world will get well soon, imag­­ining art as a medi­um of mem­ory, res­o­nance, re­cov­ery, and peace.

麥瑞顯（1996年生於香港）是跨領域藝術家及詩人，創作流動影像裝置、版畫和表演。曾在香港（2016—）和雪梨（2017）展覽、表演和出版，2018年香港大學園境學士畢業，短片作品〈tofu〉亦曾於2019年在大英博物館放映。將於2024年6月從牛津大學美術碩士課程畢業。

透過聲動力實驗，探索臨界空間中漂泊的歸屬感，將民俗、喜劇、創傷和科技交叉授粉。祈願世界早日康復，將藝術想像為記憶、共鳴、療癒及和平的媒介。
