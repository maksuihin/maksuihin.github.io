Sui-hin MAK 麥瑞顯

[ Connect on: Facebook | Instagram | Patreon ]

Sui-hin Mak (b. 1996, Hong Kong) is an artist-poet working with moving-image installations, printmaking, sound, and performance.

Mak has shown in HK, Oxford, London, Sydney, and Adelaide. Mak took science and maths in secondary school. Mak got robbed in Melbourne and hitchhiked back to HK in 2017. Mak earned a BA in Landscape Studies at HKU in 2018, where Mak learnt digital fabrication, GIS, planting, ecology, woodworking, architecture theory and history, and won photography and writing awards. Mak had fieldwork in Taiwan, Kagoshima, Laos, Tibetan areas, Dunhuang, Urumqi, et al. Mak’s short film ‘tofu’ was screened at the British Museum in 2019. Mak worked in healthcare during the SARS-CoV-2 outbreak in HK. Mak self-published a poetry riso zine ‘tinder sociology’ in 2024. Mak will soon be graduating from Oxford University with a Distinction in Master of Fine Art.

Lucid-dreaming mutedly and sonophotodynamically, Sui-hin Mak’s art practice explores a floating sense of fringe liminal belonging. Mak’s atmospheric experiences encourage viewers to feel diasporic stories and latent qualities of everyday objects, places, language, and sounds. Mak cross-pollinates identity, folklore, mysticism, comedy, trauma, technology, processes, and nature. Informed by ongoing colonialism, Mak prays that the world will get well soon, imagining art as a medium of memory, resonance, recovery, and peace.

麥瑞顯（1996年生於香港）是跨領域藝術家及詩人，創作流動影像裝置、版畫、聲音和表演。曾在香港、牛津、倫敦、雪梨和阿德萊德展演。中學修讀數理，2017年在墨爾本遇劫後截順風車流浪回港，2018年香港大學園境學士畢業，學習數位製造、地理資訊系統（GIS）、植物、生態、木工、建築理論和歷史，並贏得攝影和寫作獎項。曾到台灣、鹿兒島、寮國、藏區、敦煌、烏魯木齊等地考察。短片作品〈tofu〉亦曾於2019年在大英博物館放映。香港SARS-CoV-2爆發期間從事公共衛生。2024年自行出版《tinder 社會學》孔版油印新詩本。將從牛津大學美術碩士課程特優畢業。

藝術創作以靜默和聲光動力的清醒夢，探索邊緣和臨界空間中漂泊的歸屬感。透過創作營造氛圍的體驗，讓觀眾感受日常物件、地方、語言和聲音的本質及流散的故事。將身份、民俗、神秘主義、喜劇、創傷、科技、過程藝術和自然交叉授粉。祈願世界早日康復，將藝術想像為記憶、共鳴、療癒及和平的媒介。
