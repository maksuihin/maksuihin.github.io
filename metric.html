Water Quality Index

Mak Sui Hin

Water quality index measures the ability of the water to provide goods and services to the ecology and humans sustainably, based on a wide range of physical, chemical and biological parameters. Levels of ammonia, chloride, copper, mercury etc are measured repetitively at different locations to determine the quality of the water body.
